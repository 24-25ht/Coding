Simple Ice Cream Cake Recipe

This Ice Cream Cake recipe is the perfect classic white cake. Light and fluffy, moist and full of flavor.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 50 minutes

  • Preparation: 20 minutes

  • Cooking: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 15.25 oz chocolate cake mix

  • 1 cup water

  • 3 large eggs

  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil

  • ½ gallon chocolate ice cream

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Pre-heat the oven: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. 2.

    Mix it well: Combine chocolate cake mix, water, eggs, and oil in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth, about 2 minutes. Pour batter into the prepared baking dish.

  3. 3.

    Bake the cake: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 26 to 30 minutes. Allow to cool completely on a wired rack.

  4. 4.

    Ice Cream: Meanwhile, remove the carton paper from ice cream. Set ice cream block on its long side. Use a piece of string or dental floss to cut ice cream in half lengthwise, then place the 2 pieces side-by-side on a sheet of waxed paper

  5. 5.

    Trim it: Remove cooled cake from the baking dish and place over the ice cream slabs. Trim cake and ice cream, so the edges are flush.

  6. 6.

    Placing the cake: Place a board or serving platter over cake. Holding onto the waxed paper and board, flip ice cream cake over. Remove the waxed paper and smooth the seam between the ice cream slabs.

  7. 7.

    Freeze and decorate: Cover top with waxed paper and freeze until very firm. Decorate as desired.
